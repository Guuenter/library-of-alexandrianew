Our Method

We treat knowledge like fine whisky: distilled, aged and uncut.

1

Systematic Review

We begin with a PRISMA-guided systematic review of every major database (PubMed, PsycINFO, JSTOR).

2

Narrative Design

Findings are woven into a storyboard by award-winning science writers and fact-checked by PhD psychologists.

3

Cinematic Production

Filmed in 4K with cinema-grade colour grading, motion graphics and lossless audio mastered at Abbey Road.

4

Peer Review

Every course is peer-reviewed by at least three external academics before release. No exceptions.
